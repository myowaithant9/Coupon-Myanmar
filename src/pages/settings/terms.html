Coupon Myanmar

Coupon Myanmar app is to offer customers the ability to know coupons and promotions quickly and use them easily. This is 100% free using for customers.

You firstly have to create your own account so that we can send notifications to you about up-to-date information of our application. It will support QR code in each shop. When you go to shop, firstly you need to do scanning QR code by using Coupon Myanmar application and then use coupon easily and enjoy your food.

You will receive up-to-date notifications about new arrival coupons and promotions from Coupon Myanmar App from time to time.

Fulfillment of emails and phone number in user registration will be sent to you at the time of reservation and we may use the user's personal information for that purpose.

If you have something to change your reservation (date, time or others), please contact phone to the restaurant within one hours prior to the booking time. Changes will be completely depend on the restaurants availability and the restaurant has the right to cancel the discount if the booking time is not during the time to offer discount. When you arrive at the restaurant and your requirements (tables or rooms) is larger than originally reservation has the option to cancel and turn away your booking.
